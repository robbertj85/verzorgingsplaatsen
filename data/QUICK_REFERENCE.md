## Typical Power Requirements

| Application | Power per Point | Connection Type |
|-------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| Light truck charging | 50-150 kW | Large-scale (grootverbruik) |
| Heavy truck charging | 150-400 kW | Large-scale (grootverbruik) |
| Depot charging | 50-150 kW | Large-scale (grootverbruik) |
| Future megawatt charging | 1000+ kW | Large-scale (grootverbruik) |
| Small facility (1-2 chargers) | <240 kW | Large-scale (grootverbruik) |
| Medium facility (3-5 chargers) | 240-1000 kW | Large-scale (grootverbruik) |
| Large facility (6+ chargers) | 1000+ kW | Large-scale (grootverbruik) |

Note: Large-scale (grootverbruik) connections are required for >3×80 ampere (approximately >55 kW at 230V)

## Typical Timeline

| Phase | Duration | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Initial contact to feasibility | 1-2 months | Varies by DSO workload |
| Feasibility study | 1-3 months | Technical assessment |
| Quotation and agreement | 1-2 months | Contract negotiation |
| Design and permitting | 2-6 months | May require Rijkswaterstaat approval |
| Construction | 3-12 months | Depends on infrastructure needs |
| **TOTAL (Normal)** | **6-18 months** | In areas without congestion |
| **TOTAL (Congested)** | **18-36+ months** | May require grid upgrades |

**Recommendation: Start 12-24 months before needed operational date**

## Grid Connection Checklist

- [ ] Identify exact location (address/coordinates)
- [ ] Determine responsible DSO (use province/municipality)
- [ ] Calculate total power requirement
- [ ] Check DSO capacity maps for congestion
- [ ] Prepare site plan and technical specifications
- [ ] Contact DSO business department
- [ ] Request preliminary feasibility assessment
- [ ] Obtain Rijkswaterstaat approval (if on motorway property)
- [ ] Review quotation and connection agreement
- [ ] Arrange metering service provider (required for grootverbruik)
- [ ] Coordinate construction timing
- [ ] Plan for commissioning and testing

## Cost Factors to Consider

**One-Time Connection Costs:**
- Distance to nearest connection point
- Required transformer capacity
- New cables and infrastructure
- Grid reinforcement requirements
- Design and project management
- **Typical range: €10,000 - €500,000+**

**Ongoing Annual Costs:**
- Grid capacity fees (transport tarieven)
- Metering services
- Maintenance
- **Typical range: €5,000 - €50,000+ per year**

**Per kWh Costs:**
- Electricity consumption (energy costs)
- **Typical range: €0.10 - €0.30 per kWh depending on time-of-use**

## Technical Optimization Tips

1. **Load Management** - Install smart charging system to balance loads
2. **Battery Storage** - Add batteries for peak shaving and backup
3. **Flexibility Contracts** - Negotiate load curtailment agreements with DSO
4. **Phased Deployment** - Start small, expand as demand grows
5. **Future-Proofing** - Plan infrastructure for 2× current capacity needs

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Starting too late** - Grid connections take 6-18+ months
2. **Underestimating power needs** - Plan for growth and simultaneous charging
3. **Ignoring congestion** - Check capacity maps before site selection
4. **Forgetting permits** - Rijkswaterstaat approval needed for motorway property
5. **Not planning for metering** - Grootverbruik requires separate metering arrangement
6. **Overlooking ongoing costs** - Grid capacity fees can be substantial
